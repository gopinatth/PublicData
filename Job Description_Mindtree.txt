Project Objective

 

Equipment finance is a division of First-Citizens Bank & Trust Company and carries out tasks of developing, maintaining and supporting number of applications which interact with third party vendor applications which provide services like credit check, maintain the Credit Application Origination Systems, Account Payable Interface system(API System) provides a detailed reporting to vendors of funds remitted for assets, Asset Management Global(AMG) that manages the assets which are returned by vendors when lease and contracts end and also facilitate the auction process for the leased assets, Managed File Transfer System (MFT) manages secure transfer of data to the various banks and vendors (which includes ACH payment files, employee information, DTCC reporting, trading data, asset information, contract details, sales reports etc.). MFT system encrypts and decrypts, digitally signs the files and transfer/receives the files to internal and external systems.

As an ESS (Enterprise Solution and Services) team at First-Citizens Bank & Trust Company, we focused on building and maintaining the services for the Equipment Finance business across the USA region. Other software components across different business lines interact with our ESS software components like Advisory service, ESF services & B2B services.
